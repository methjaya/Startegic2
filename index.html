Executive Summary

Core Strategy

Focused Differentiation. Positioned as a premium functional beverage, not a commodity.

Target Market

Middle East (UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia) targeting health-conscious consumers.

Entry Mode

Direct Exporting via regional distributors to maintain low risk and high flexibility.

Value Prop

Natural, organic electrolytes and "Pure Origin" branding from Sri Lanka.

Competitive Advantage Framework

Firm-Specific (Internal)

  • Direct farm-to-export sourcing network
  • Cold-chain logistics & HACCP certification
  • Premium "Pure Origin" brand identity

Geographic (Sri Lanka)

  • Native Origin: Thambili is unique to Sri Lanka
  • Ideal climate for 365-day production cycle
  • Strategic location for ME/Asia trade routes

Export Strategy
